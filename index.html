Where to begin?

Several steps how to start drawing

1. Starting drawing. (zero stage) In the best traditions of the old academies, the student at the first stage copies the "original" educational drawing.

2. Geometric shapes. Mastering the skill of drawing geometric shapes (cube, prism, cylinder, ball, etc.). The alternation of long work on the production of several figures and work on sketches of one figure.

3. Sketching is a quick sketching technique. This skill helps to convey your idea to others in detail and in an accessible way for further implementation.
